üìñ Key Definitions (BL2025-1116)

üîå

Electric Vehicle Charging Station

A site or facility that is accessory to another principal use and contains one or more vehicle stalls for charging EVs using EVSE and associated infrastructure.

‚ö°

Level 2 EVSE

Electric vehicle supply equipment utilizing alternating current (AC) with output power range of 3kW - 22kW.

üîã

DCFC EVSE

Direct Current Fast Charging equipment utilizing direct current (DC) with output power of 50kW or greater.
